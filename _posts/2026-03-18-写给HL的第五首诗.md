Meeting Spring
What I met in Spring, it's a secret
to say or describe it, that's so difficult
All of blossoms smiling, except me
Till got the sweet, so regret feeling!

Wind is fleeing, sun closing
ice melts, shade waves under trees
Standing on the bridge, all things like a dream
Have met the Spring, no found my Spring!

What I met in Spring, there's no answer
In a word,
My Spring is disappearing.

鸢夜  3.16-3.18.
